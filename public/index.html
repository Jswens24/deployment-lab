Helmets Save Lives!

  • It is estimated that 75% of bicycle-related fatalities among children could be prevented with a bicycle helmet.
  • Riders injured using E-bikes were more likely to suffer internal injuries (17.1%) and require hospital admission
  • Nine of 10 support helmet laws for children, while 62% support such laws for adults.
  • Powered scooter injuries were nearly three times more likely to result in a diagnosis of concussion (3% of scooter injuries vs 0.5% of E-bike injuries)
  • About 4% of bicyclists or 2.04 million, were injured while riding in the past two years. About .5 million of these were hit by a motorist.
